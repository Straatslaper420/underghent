New lib pull-venues-from-sheet.ts + command pull-venues-from-sheet.ts.
Merges by id: matching venues are updated in place (file order preserved), rows with a new id are appended, and local venues missing from the sheet are kept, never deleted — so a stray blank row can't wipe data.
